────────────────────────────────────────
0. BRAND SYSTEM — DEFINE THE VISUAL LANGUAGE
────────────────────────────────────────

Implement a unified design system:

**COLORS**
- primary: #FFFFFF
- black: #000000 or #0C0C0C (matte black)
- gold: #C7A556 (premium metallic gold — refine if necessary)
- greys: minimal, neutral greys for spacing, dividers, and secondary text

**TYPOGRAPHY**
- Use a modern, premium sans-serif font (e.g. Inter, Satoshi, or IBM Plex Sans)
- Headings: tight letter-spacing, strong hierarchy
- Body: light, unobtrusive, readable

**SPACING & GRID**
- Use a deliberate spacing scale (4 / 8 / 12 / 16 / 24 / 32 / 48 px)
- All content aligns to a consistent grid layout
- Use whitespace generously (Apple-like lightness)

**COMPONENT SHAPE**
- Consistent radius: 10–12px
- Subtle shadows only where needed
- Inputs & buttons with defined height, spacing, and iconography

**INTERACTION DESIGN**
- Buttons: gold background, black text OR black with gold text
- Hover states: subtle elevation, smooth transition
- Tabs: underline or indicator in gold
- Cards: white background, gold border or accent stripe
- Icons: black with gold highlights where appropriate

All components must feel intentional and premium.
